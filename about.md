The **DraCor Blog** is the news and writing outlet of the [DraCor](https://dracor.org) project ("drama corpora"), an open digital infrastructure for the computational study of drama.

This blog is produced in the context of the [OSCARS](https://oscars-project.eu/) project, an EU-funded initiative to foster the uptake of Open Science in Europe. Its primary focus is **DraCorOS** — our work on Open Science workflows around DraCor corpora, tooling, and the platform itself.

Occasional cross-posts from other DraCor team members and venues will be clearly marked as such.
